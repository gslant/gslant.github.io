Opportunities for uses of AI in surveillance

There are many types of surveillance, not all of them bad. AI can massively improve the effectiveness of surveillance systems

One promising use-case for surveillance-AI is in medical imaging. AI can be used to model disease transmission, through a method called syndromic surveillance. Syndromic surveillance is a technique used by medical professionals to gather and monitor statistical data such as disease markers, hospital case data. (Chen et al. 2009) on effective use of surveillance systems, says "There are circumstances in which automated disease surveillance might not be effective or necessary.
