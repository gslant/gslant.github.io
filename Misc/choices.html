Potential Choices We Face With AI Technology

When specifically talking about AI surveilience, there are really only two options to choose between; Do we use it to monitor people for the safety of the people, or do we use it to monitor people to further a government agenda? I have named these choices "The constructive route" and "The destructive route" respectively.

The Constructive Route:

The idea behind the constructive route is to make society a safer, more convenient place to live through the use of AI surveilience. The most interesting example of this is the product "Ella", being developed by "IC Realtime". 'Ella' is a program applied to security cameras that allows you to "google" for specific events, objects, etc.. that has been captured by the video camera. To put it in IC Realtime's words:

(Ella | Video Search Engine, 2021) "It can handle 3 video streams @ 2mp or 2 video streams @ 4MP.It's more than just a cloud storage stream box; an Ai assisted search engine, or a network load reducer. Ella is an optimized implementation of all three concepts - in one tiny package that makes your video security into the fast, efficient resource it was always meant to be."

Ella was created as a safety tool. An example they use to describe Ella's ability is being able to identify a Jeep Wrangler which was used as a get-away vehicle after a robbery. - This example is hypothetical, however, it makes it clear that Ella's developers are desinging this tool with good intention, as they want Ella to serve society in a constructive way.
IC Realtime arent the only people who believe AI surveilience can help prevent crimes. When explaining the benefits of AI, (Feldstein 2019) said:

"...tracking tools play a vital role in preventing terrorism. They help security forces deter bad acts and resolve problematic cases. They give authoritie the ability to monitor critical threats and react accordingly."

Understanably, Feldstein doesn't go much more in-depth into classified counter-terroism operations, but we can assume that technologies similar to Ella, and facial-recognition are being used to identify and help respond to threats. This is, in essence, the ideal use of AI surveilience.
